The Best TV Shows According to TJ

My Top 3 Shows of all-time.

1. Friends

The show is very relatable, quotable, and funny!
I would also consider it my "comfort show".

2. The Sons of Anarchy

This show has an INCREDIBLE story. It is quite brutal and wouldn't be suitable for all people, though

3. The Good Place

This show is very fun and has a gripping story that will make you laugh & cry.
Despite it's subject, though, it is not a "Christian" show nor does it have theology that aligns with the Christian faith. Keep this in mind when watching or referring the show to a friend.
